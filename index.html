Mark Haddon's novel The Curious Incident of the Dog in the Night Time is a first-person coming-of-age novel that takes the shape of a mystery investigation. The novel is also unique in that the narrator is probably autistic, although this is never explicitly stated in the story. In addition to being a fast-paced and interesting read, the novel encourages readers to view everyday situations from a new perspective. For this reason, the novel is a good discussion-starter.
